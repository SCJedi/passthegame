# Pry

A top-down 2D maze game where you escape rooms by **prying** your way out with collected tools. Early levels are single-exit tutorials that teach one item at a time; later levels are open mazes with obstacles you can bypass multiple ways using anything you've learned.

- **Genre:** top-down 2D puzzle-maze
- **Vibe:** minimalist, MacGyver-in-a-room, "aha" moments
- **Core verb:** PRY — use the right tool to defeat an obstacle and reach the exit
